PROJECTS

ProTag

Music almanac web app that leverages the Genius API. With interactive D3 and Chart.js data visualizations, users are given an innovative way to explore the creation of their favorite music and artists.

Tech Stack: Python, JavaScript, AJAX, HTML5, CSS3, JSON, SQL, Flask, jQuery, Bootstrap, scikit-learn

Mobility

Mobility provides users with data visualizations of the frequency of bikes and scooters in US's main regions. Mobility aggregates all GBFS feeds to enable developers to build data visualizations.

Tech Stack: Python, JavaScript, AJAX, SQL, Flask, jQuery, kepler.gl

We the People

Social network addressing the 16th sustainable development goal: Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions. Users, referred to as citizens, can portfolio their accomplishments for social good and visualize that data. Via a JIRA-like “ticket” system, users can assign initiatives to politicians, whom also have profiles, neighbors, or themselves.

Tech Stack: Python, JavaScript, AJAX, HTML5, CSS3, JSON, SQL, Flask, jQuery, Bootstrap
